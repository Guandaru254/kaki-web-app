OUR HISTORY

KaKi Cleaning Company was established in 2022 by two young visionaries, Victor Kamau and Dedan Kimathi.Initially focused on laundry services, the company rapidly diversified its offerings to include carpet cleaning, office cleaning, and apartment cleaning.The team has also grown from the initial founders to include other departments like marketing, sales, tech, finance among others

We are very greatful for the far we've come and we know that the future is brighter.

Our Core Values Services

OUR MISSION

To revolutionize the cleaning industry by seamlessly blending cutting-edge technology, sustainable practices, and forward-thinking innovation, while delivering exceptional service to our clients.

OUR VISION

We envision a future where cleaning is not just a chore, but a seamless and sustainable experience powered by technology and driven by a commitment to environmental responsibility and community impact. Through our innovative approach, we aim to set new standards for service excellence, inspire positive change in our industry, and empower individuals of all ages to lead with purpose and passion.

COMPANY CULTURE

  • Innovation and Technology-Driven: Foster a culture of innovation where employees are encouraged to embrace and harness the power of technology to enhance efficiency, streamline processes, and improve service delivery. Encourage a mindset of continuous learning and adaptation to stay at the forefront of technological advancements in the cleaning industry.
  • Sustainability and Environmental Responsibility: Promote a commitment to sustainability by integrating eco-friendly practices into all aspects of the business operations. Encourage recycling, energy conservation, and the use of environmentally friendly cleaning products to minimize the company's carbon footprint and contribute to a healthier planet.
  • Customer-Centric Approach: Prioritize customer satisfaction by delivering timely and high-quality service that exceeds expectations. Empower employees to go above and beyond to meet the needs of clients, anticipate their preferences, and provide personalized solutions that demonstrate care and attention to detail.
  • Community Engagement and Social Impact: Cultivate a culture of community engagement and social responsibility by actively participating in initiatives that benefit the local community and society at large. Encourage employees to volunteer, participate in charity events, and contribute to causes that align with the company's values, such as education, health, or environmental conservation.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: Embrace diversity and foster an inclusive work environment where all employees feel valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives and talents. Promote diversity in hiring practices, provide equal opportunities for growth and development, and celebrate the rich tapestry of backgrounds, cultures, and experiences within the company.
  • Collaboration and Teamwork: Encourage collaboration and teamwork by fostering a supportive and inclusive culture where employees collaborate across departments, share knowledge and expertise, and work together towards common goals. Recognize and celebrate collective achievements, and encourage open communication, feedback, and idea-sharing among team members.
  • Flexibility and Work-Life Balance: Promote a healthy work-life balance by offering flexible work arrangements, remote work options, and supportive policies that allow employees to prioritize their well-being and personal commitments. Encourage a culture of trust and autonomy, where employees have the freedom to manage their time and responsibilities effectively.
